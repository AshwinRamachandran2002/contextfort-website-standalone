ContextFort Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 6, 2026

What We Collect

Local Storage (on your device):

  • Screenshots captured during AI agent sessions
  • URLs and page titles visited by the agent
  • User interactions (clicks, inputs) during agent mode
  • Session data (timestamps, duration, status)
  • Your blocking rules and configuration settings

Analytics (sent to PostHog):

  • Extension usage events (installation, updates)
  • Feature usage (when blocking rules are triggered)
  • Anonymous session counts and durations
  • No personally identifiable information
  • No screenshots, URLs, or page content

When We Collect

Data collection only occurs when Claude AI agent mode is detected on a webpage (identifiable by the glowing border UI element). No monitoring happens during normal browsing.

Where Data is Stored

  • Local data: Stored on your device using chrome.storage.local - never transmitted to our servers
  • Analytics data: Sent to PostHog (posthog.com) for product improvement
  • No cloud storage of screenshots or browsing data

What We Don't Collect

  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health information
  • Your location or IP address
  • Keystrokes when agent is not active
  • Data from personal communications

Third-Party Services

PostHog Analytics: We use PostHog for anonymous usage analytics. View their privacy policy at https://posthog.com/privacy

Your Rights

  • View your data: Use the extension dashboard to review all stored sessions
  • Delete your data: Clear data through the extension or browser settings
  • Opt-out: Uninstall the extension to stop all data collection

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not share screenshots or URLs with anyone
  • Only anonymous analytics shared with PostHog for product improvement

Note: This extension is designed for monitoring AI agent behavior. Use responsibly and avoid running AI agents on sensitive sites (banking, healthcare) while this extension is active.
